Puppies

Puppies are tiny bundles of joy with wobbly legs, floppy ears, and endless curiosity.
Born with their eyes and ears shut tight, they spend their first few weeks snuggled up in a puppy pile, learning how to be part of a pack.
As they grow, they develop sharp little baby teeth perfect for nibbling toes and tugging on toys.
With every tumble and tail chase, they’re not just playing—they're learning how to communicate and be adorable doggos-in-training. Basically, puppies are furry chaos wrapped in pure happiness!

  • Puppies are born blind and deaf.
  • They have baby teeth.
  • Puppies learn social skills from their littermates.
